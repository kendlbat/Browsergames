Browsergames

Made by Tobias Kendlbacher

What this project is about:

I wanted to have some fun with programming, so I decided to make some games.
First it was only for my own amusement, but after a while I decided to make it public.

I hope you enjoy it as much as I did creating it!

  • Sudoku

    Players: 1

    Modes: Easy, Medium, Hard and Random

    Objective: Fill the board so all numbers are unique in each row, column and 3x3 area.

    Difficulty: Variable

  • Minesweeper

    Players: 1

    Modes: Easy, Medium and Hard

    Objective: Reveal all non-mine cells without exploding a mine!

    Difficulty: Variable

  • Pong

    Players: 1 / 2

    Modes: You can play against an AI or your friends!

    Objective: Don't let the ball touch your wall!

    Difficulty: Easy

  • Snake

    Players: 1

    Modes: You can only play alone :(

    Objective: Eat as many apples as you can! (Also don't bump into anything.)

    Difficulty: Medium

  • Connect 4

    Players: 2

    Modes: You can only play against your friends!

    Objective: Be the first with 4 pieces in a row (column or diagonal)!

    Difficulty: Easy
